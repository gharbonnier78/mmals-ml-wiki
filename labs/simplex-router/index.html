Router on a simplex

Change three host preferences and watch the normalized route move through the probability simplex.

concept: probability-simplexteaching simulation
For three hosts, a route vector lives inside a triangle. Corners are single-host routes; the center is equal sharing.
entropy
dominance
effective hosts
dominant host

What to notice

Entropy is highest near the center and falls toward a corner. Dominance rises as one host receives most of the route mass. Neither proves that the dominant host is useful; contribution and ablation must be checked separately.
