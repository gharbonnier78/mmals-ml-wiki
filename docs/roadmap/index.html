Roadmap

Planned evolution from first encyclopedia to governed research layer.

v0.1.0 — foundation

Canonical concepts, concept graph, six labs, article link contract, and link-health automation.

v0.2.0 — research traceability

Repository and paper registry, claim-to-artifact links, evidence ZIP manifests, frozen snapshots, and notebook backlinks.

v0.3.0 — richer interaction

Searchable glossary, bilingual EN/FR content, interactive evidence timelines, dataset geometry labs, and accessibility review.

v1.0.0 — governed knowledge layer

Schema validation, release-to-encyclopedia automation, reviewer-facing bundles, semantic versioning of claims, and reproducible citation snapshots.
