Authoring guide

How to write a canonical Diderot concept page.

Concept-page contract

  1. One-sentence definition
  2. Why it matters
  3. Discover explanation
  4. Engineering explanation
  5. Research explanation
  6. Formal anchor
  7. Current evidence
  8. Boundary or non-claim
  9. Related concepts
  10. Stable publication link
  11. Last-reviewed date

Reading levels

Discover

Analogy, minimal vocabulary, visual intuition, and what to notice.

Engineer

Architecture, algorithm, metrics, implementation consequences, and observability.

Research

Assumptions, formalization, evidence design, ablations, and limitations.

Promotion rule

Do not promote a page because the prose is persuasive. Promote it only when the evidence ledger changes.
