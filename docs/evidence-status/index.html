Evidence status taxonomy

How Diderot distinguishes mathematics, implementation, evidence, and hypotheses.

StatusMeaning
FoundationalStable mathematics or canonical project semantics.
ImplementedPresent in a notebook, package, or code path.
Smoke testedExecuted only as an integrity or feasibility check.
Multi-seed evidenceRepeated under a declared protocol across seeds.
Mechanistic supportDiagnostic or causal evidence supports the proposed mechanism.
Negative resultA declared hypothesis or policy did not obtain the expected result.
Open hypothesisA testable direction without sufficient evidence.
Speculative branchPreserved for exploration and clearly outside the validated core.
The public badge states the strongest defensible claim, not the most ambitious aspiration.
