Semantic contract

Canonical biological and computational vocabulary.

Canonical biological mapping

Hosts are the trees or host organisms. The fungal medium is the mediating mycelial system. Mycorrhiza is the mutualistic exchange interface or relationship. These are three distinct objects.

Naming discipline

A biological word must map to an explicit computational object, metric, or hypothesis. When no such object exists, ordinary machine-learning vocabulary is preferable.
