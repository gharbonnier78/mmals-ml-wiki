Research narrative

From mycelial intuition to auditable adaptive learning

The MMALS story is not a straight line of model improvements. It is a sequence of hypotheses, counterexamples, branch separations, and progressively stricter evidence contracts.

01

From a biological analogy to a falsifiable architecture

The starting point was not “copy nature and expect intelligence.” It was a disciplined mapping: hosts remain the organisms, the fungal system is the mediating network, and mycorrhiza is the governed exchange relationship. That distinction produced testable components—specialists, transformations, routes, costs, contribution, and memory.

02

The prototype learns to correct itself

v0.1 asked whether persistent paths could reduce forgetting. v0.2 exposed capacity and freezing confounds. v0.3 and v0.4 made the medium active but found unstable mutualistic pressure. The program preserved these corrections instead of rewriting the story as uninterrupted success.

03

The route is not the function

v0.5 through v0.9 established the central conceptual shift. A route can remain stable while host representations or transformations drift. Functional memory therefore targets the mediated latent computation and downstream behavior, not only route coefficients.

04

Context must be inferred

The v1.0 transition removed the assumption that the correct task identity is known. MMALS first estimates q(c|x), then routes and acts. This created a new decomposition: context competence, routing competence, output competence, calibration, and action safety.

05

One trunk becomes a research ecosystem

RC2O broadened continual-learning evidence. Geometry-MMALS studied route and memory geometry. Stable–plastic context memory addressed rescue failures. CAL governed uncertainty and action. TPUT and goal-adaptive control connected objectives, cost, energy, and system-level contribution.

06

Research becomes a governed public object

GitHub packages made branches independently reviewable. The Science Magazine added an illustrated narrative. Diderot now connects concepts, events, branches, evidence, repositories, and publication status while preserving negative results and historical claim boundaries.

What this story deliberately preserves

  • negative and mixed results;
  • changes in the meaning of “memory”;
  • the transition from explicit task IDs to inferred context;
  • the difference between a mechanism contribution and a performance policy;
  • the separation between core evidence and adjacent inspiration.
