Program architecture

Branches of the MMALS research program

A branch map that distinguishes the evidence-bearing core, diagnostic and future hypotheses, control and evaluation layers, pedagogy, and adjacent machine-learning or governance projects.

Select a node to jump to its branch record. Lines indicate program ancestry or dependency, not proof of causal success.

Boundary rule: adjacent projects may contribute methods, governance, or inspiration, but they do not become MMALS evidence unless an explicit experiment and evidence contract connect them.
