Important boundary

The public chronicle is curated, not a chat dump

Working conversations may contain provisional ideas, errors, private context, and repeated reformulations. Diderot publishes research events only after they are summarized, assigned a date and branch, linked to an artifact where possible, and given an explicit evidence status.
