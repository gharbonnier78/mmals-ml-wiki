6 May 2026
originMMALS core trunkcurated project record

The founding question

Could a learning system be designed from the disciplined analogy of hosts, a mediating fungal network, and mycorrhizal exchange rather than from a neuron-only metaphor?

Research consequence

A research program is framed around active mediation, mutual benefit, dynamic topology, and structural memory.

Provenance: curated project conversation and source thread

v0.1 — persistent structural routing

The first reduced experiment asks whether dynamic, sparse, persistent routing can reduce forgetting on a toy sequential benchmark.

Research consequence

A falsifiable minimum experiment replaces a broad biological claim.

Provenance: MMALS Prototype 0.1 notebook
